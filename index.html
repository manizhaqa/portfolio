About Me

I like the part of QA where curiosity becomes clarity.

My background is in SaaS product testing, billing workflows, analytics, integrations, and release validation. I am comfortable sitting in the messy middle of requirements, product questions, edge cases, and technical details until the team has a clear path forward.

I care about writing bug reports that are easy to act on, building test coverage that matches real risk, and helping Product and Engineering teams understand what is ready, what is risky, and what still needs attention.

A few things to know

  1. I lead with manual testing and product judgment.
  2. I use automation where it makes testing faster, clearer, or more repeatable.
  3. I use AI to speed up test planning, edge-case brainstorming, and documentation.
  4. I do not let tools replace my responsibility to validate behavior and communicate risk.
  5. I enjoy working closely with Product, Engineering, and stakeholders before issues reach users.
